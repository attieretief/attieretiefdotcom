Love & longing

Fighting

by Attie Retief
Poetry · English

The thought of you so clouds my mind
whenever I think of joy:
so strong, so vivid, so kind -
and yet, even now as I grow
in the love of you
I may never come to know
the sweet taste of your lips
nor the soft touch
your soothing hands
may bring.
