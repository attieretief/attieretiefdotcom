Victoria Falls

by Attie Retief
Poetry · English

A scenic phenomenon - this domain of God.
Mozi o Tunya,
Manza Thungayo,
Unrivalled majesty.

Great masses of pouring water
falls into
a deep trench
and the nightshadows fade into a harmony of colours
(the ghosts of the river will soon play with a lunar rainbow)

And the water boils,
uttering a raging
cloud of
spray...

rising smoke
