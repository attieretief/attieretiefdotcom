God in you

Poetry · English

If God is forgiveness
Then you are my cross

If God is resurrection
If God is resurrection
Then you are my third day
Then you are my third day

If God is righteousness
If God is righteousness
If God is righteousness
Then you are my judge

If God is creation
Then you are my world
