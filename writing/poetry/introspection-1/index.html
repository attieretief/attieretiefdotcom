Self & shadow

Introspection 1

by Attie Retief
Poetry · English

And even now as I discover the depth of my soul,
I still fear the dark infinity it bears.

For introspection, into the dark and gloomy hole
of one's deepest hopes and fears,

is a mystery which leads only
to the realization of one's incapability
to comprehend
the full extent
of it all.
