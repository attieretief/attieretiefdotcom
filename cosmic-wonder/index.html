Superfluid Vacuum Theology

What if the deepest equation in mathematics and the oldest story in theology are saying the same thing in different languages?

This book walks patiently through modern physics — from the grammar of light to quantum fields to the superfluid vacuum hypothesis — and discovers that Euler's identity, eiπ + 1 = 0, maps structurally onto the arc of creation, fall, incarnation, and restoration.

Not proof. Resonance. The kind that makes you stop and listen.
